Maldives

Maldives, officially the Republic of Maldives, and historically known as the Maldive Islands, is an archipelagic country in South Asia, located in the eastern Arabian Sea, within the northern Indian Ocean.

Parque de la Barceloneta

Parque de la Barceloneta (often referred to as Parc de la Barceloneta) is a lively, 24-hour urban green space near the beach, featuring modern recreational amenities

Blue Lagoon Island

Blue Lagoon Island (Salt Cay) is a private island near Nassau, Bahamas, famous for its turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and the Dolphin Encounters marine park.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a tropical Indian Union Territory located in the Bay of Bengal, consisting of 836 islands (31 inhabited) famed for white sand beaches, coral reefs, and scuba diving

Finland

Finland, officially the Republic of Finland, is a Nordic country in Northern Europe with a population of 5.7 million and its capital in Helsinki

Helsinki

Helsinki, the capital and largest city of Finland, is a vibrant, coastal city known as the "Daughter of the Baltic" and renowned for its high quality of life, functional Nordic design, and seamless blend of nature and urban living.
